## Summary
The post argues that many B2B companies overspend on paid search because they focus on misleading metrics instead of revenue outcomes. It recommends measuring campaign success using revenue generated and high-intent pipeline relative to ad spend, then evaluating performance by campaign type to identify which campaigns truly deliver ROI.

Key Takeaways:

Measure paid search success using revenue, not just clicks or conversions.
Track two key metrics:
Closed Won Revenue : Ad Spend (actual ROI)
HIRO Pipeline : Ad Spend (future revenue potential)
