## Summary

In this video, Richard van der Blom explains how the LinkedIn algorithm works and how creators and B2B SaaS professionals can improve the reach of their organic content. He discusses the importance of publishing high-quality, audience-focused posts, encouraging meaningful engagement, and maintaining consistency. The video also covers best practices for increasing visibility while building long-term credibility on LinkedIn.

## Key Takeaways

- Understand how the LinkedIn algorithm evaluates content.
- Create valuable, audience-focused posts instead of promotional content.
- Encourage meaningful conversations through comments and discussions.
- Publish consistently to improve visibility and audience trust.
- Use relevant formats such as text posts, carousels, and videos when appropriate.
- Monitor engagement metrics to refine your content strategy.
- Build relationships through authentic interactions with your network.
- Align LinkedIn content with B2B SaaS marketing objectives.
