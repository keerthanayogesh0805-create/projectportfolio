- **Channel:** Ross Simmonds

## Summary

In this video, Ross Simmonds explains how B2B companies can use LinkedIn as an organic growth channel. He emphasizes creating valuable content that educates the audience, building trust through consistency, and distributing content effectively to increase reach and engagement. The discussion also highlights the importance of understanding the target audience and measuring content performance to improve future strategies.

## Key Takeaways

- Create content that solves your audience's problems.
- Publish consistently to build trust and visibility.
- Focus on quality and value rather than promotional content.
- Repurpose content into multiple formats for wider reach.
- Encourage conversations by asking questions and responding to comments.
- Analyze content performance to identify what resonates with your audience.
- Build long-term relationships through authentic engagement.
- Align content with business goals and customer needs.
