## Summary

In this podcast, Amanda Natividad discusses how B2B SaaS companies can build an effective LinkedIn content strategy by creating educational, audience-focused content. She explains the importance of understanding customer pain points, publishing consistently, and measuring content performance to improve engagement and brand awareness. The conversation also highlights how authentic storytelling and valuable insights help build trust with professional audiences.

## Key Takeaways

- Create educational content that addresses audience needs.
- Focus on delivering value rather than promoting products.
- Publish consistently to build credibility and visibility.
- Use storytelling to make content more engaging.
- Understand your target audience before planning content.
- Monitor engagement metrics to improve future posts.
- Encourage meaningful conversations through comments.
- Align LinkedIn content with overall B2B SaaS marketing objectives.
