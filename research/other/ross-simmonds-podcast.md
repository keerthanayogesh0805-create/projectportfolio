# Ross Simmonds Podcast: LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Podcast Information

- **Expert:** Ross Simmonds
- **Episode:** LinkedIn Content Strategy & Content Distribution
- **Platform:** YouTube Podcast
- **Link:** https://youtu.be/9AEShQj4OaA
- **Topic:** LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Summary

In this podcast, Ross Simmonds discusses how B2B SaaS companies can use LinkedIn as a powerful organic marketing channel. He explains the importance of creating valuable content, distributing it effectively, and building trust with the target audience. The conversation also covers content repurposing, audience engagement, and measuring performance to improve long-term content marketing results.

## Key Takeaways

- Create valuable content that solves customer problems.
- Use LinkedIn consistently to build authority and trust.
- Repurpose content into multiple formats to maximize reach.
- Focus on meaningful engagement instead of promotional posts.
- Build a long-term content distribution strategy.
- Understand your audience before creating content.
- Track content performance and optimize future posts.
- Align LinkedIn content with B2B SaaS marketing goals.
