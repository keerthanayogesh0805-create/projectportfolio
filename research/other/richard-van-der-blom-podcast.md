## Podcast Information

- **Expert:** Richard van der Blom
- **Episode:** LinkedIn Algorithm & Organic Content Strategy
- **Platform:** YouTube Podcast

- **Topic:** LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Summary

In this podcast, Richard van der Blom explains how the LinkedIn algorithm influences content visibility and engagement. He discusses best practices for creating high-quality, audience-focused content, maintaining consistent posting habits, and encouraging meaningful interactions. The conversation also explores how B2B SaaS professionals can improve their LinkedIn strategy by understanding audience behavior, monitoring analytics, and continuously refining their content approach.

## Key Takeaways

- Understand how the LinkedIn algorithm ranks and distributes content.
- Create audience-first content that provides practical value.
- Post consistently to build visibility and long-term credibility.
- Encourage meaningful conversations through comments and engagement.
- Monitor analytics to improve future content performance.
- Build relationships through authentic interactions.
- Use a mix of content formats to increase reach.
- Align LinkedIn content with overall B2B SaaS marketing goals.

Richard van der Blom is one of the leading researchers on the LinkedIn algorithm. His insights help B2B SaaS marketers understand how to improve organic reach, increase engagement, and build a sustainable LinkedIn content strategy.
