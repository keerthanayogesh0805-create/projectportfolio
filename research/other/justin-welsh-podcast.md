# Justin Welsh Podcast: Building a Personal Brand on LinkedIn

## Podcast Information

- **Expert:** Justin Welsh
- **Episode:** Personal Branding & LinkedIn Growth
- **Platform:** YouTube Podcast
- **Link:** https://youtu.be/AJinaz2C74k
- **Topic:** LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Summary

In this podcast, Justin Welsh shares his approach to building a successful personal brand on LinkedIn through consistent, value-driven content. He explains how creators and B2B SaaS professionals can grow an audience by understanding customer needs, sharing practical insights, and maintaining authenticity. The discussion also covers creating repeatable content systems, leveraging AI responsibly, and building trust over time.

## Key Takeaways

- Build a personal brand by consistently publishing valuable content.
- Focus on solving your audience's problems rather than promoting products.
- Develop a repeatable content creation system.
- Stay authentic to build long-term trust and credibility.
- Use AI to improve productivity while maintaining originality.
- Engage with your audience through meaningful conversations.
- Measure content performance and refine your strategy.
- Think long-term when building your LinkedIn presence.

Justin Welsh is a leading expert in LinkedIn growth, personal branding, and content strategy. His practical advice is highly relevant to understanding how B2B SaaS professionals can use LinkedIn as an organic marketing channel.
