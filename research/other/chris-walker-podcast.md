## Summary

In this podcast, Chris Walker discusses how B2B SaaS companies can build sustainable growth through demand generation and organic content marketing. He explains why businesses should focus on educating their audience, creating valuable LinkedIn content, and building trust instead of relying solely on promotional campaigns. The discussion also covers audience engagement, measuring marketing performance, and developing long-term content strategies.

## Key Takeaways

- Focus on demand generation rather than direct selling.
- Create educational content that addresses customer challenges.
- Use LinkedIn to build trust and establish industry authority.
- Publish consistently to improve visibility and audience engagement.
- Understand customer needs before creating content.
- Measure marketing performance to optimize future strategies.
- Encourage meaningful discussions through audience engagement.
- Align LinkedIn content with overall B2B SaaS business goals.
