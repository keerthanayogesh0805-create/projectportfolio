## Podcast Information

- **Expert:** Dave Gerhardt

## Summary

In this podcast, Dave Gerhardt discusses how B2B SaaS companies can use LinkedIn to strengthen their brand, build trust, and engage their target audience through organic content. He explains the importance of creating authentic content, understanding customer needs, and maintaining consistency. The conversation also covers personal branding, audience engagement, and measuring content performance to improve marketing results.

## Key Takeaways

- Build a strong personal and company brand through LinkedIn.
- Share authentic and educational content instead of promotional posts.
- Understand your audience before planning content.
- Publish consistently to increase visibility and engagement.
- Encourage meaningful conversations through comments and interactions.
- Use storytelling to make content more relatable.
- Measure content performance using analytics.
- Align LinkedIn content with long-term B2B SaaS marketing goals.
